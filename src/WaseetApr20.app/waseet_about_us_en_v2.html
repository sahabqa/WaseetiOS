Waseet App

Waseet App allows you to browse thousands of ads in different sections either cars for sale, real estate for sale or rent. If you are a job seeker, Waseet's app will help you to find thousands of job vacancies, in addition to ability to sell your personal stuff like Mobile, Camera, Watches, laptops, and other stuff like electronics and accessories.

Through the new Waseet app you can:
1- Signup quickly through using your mobile number.
2- Place your ad quickly and add your photos to it in seconds.
3- Browse tens of thousands of ads in a simple way allowing you to find what you're looking for in a few clicks.
4- Search for ads by entering your keyword and get accurate results.
5- Multiple options to contact the owner of the ad you're interested in.
6- Share any ad that you find interesting on WhatsApp and other social media sites.
7- Control and edit or unpublish your ads from your profile area.
